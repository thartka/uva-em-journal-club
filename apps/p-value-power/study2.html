Study 2: The BREEZE Trial

BREEZE Trial: Bronchiolitis Respiratory Evaluation of Efficacy with Zero Escalation

A randomized controlled trial comparing high-flow nasal cannula (HFNC) vs. standard nasal cannula for bronchiolitis in the emergency department.

Primary outcome: Escalation of care (ICU transfer or intubation) within 24 hours.

Design: 100 patients per arm (200 total), α = 0.05 (two-sided), power = 80% to detect a 15 percentage point absolute difference in escalation rates (25% vs 10%).
