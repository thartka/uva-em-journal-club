Study 1: The PANDA Trial

PANDA Trial: Pediatric Antiemetic — Novel Delivery Assessment

A randomized controlled trial comparing IV ondansetron vs. oral dissolving tablet ondansetron for reducing vomiting in pediatric gastroenteritis.

Primary outcome: Additional vomiting episode within 2 hours of treatment.

Design: 100 patients per arm (200 total), α = 0.05 (two-sided), power = 80%.
